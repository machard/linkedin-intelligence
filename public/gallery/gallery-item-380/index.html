Some thoughts behind the meme Bitcoin was designed to be apolitical, but capital flows are never neutral. When feeder mechanisms (like Tether or Saylor) push billions into BTC — and mining is concentrated in energyrich, statecontrolled territories — we should ask Where does the loop go? Ethereum, despite its messiness, chooses to move forward. Coordination under constraint is harder — but more human.
