In Ethereum, you're not bound by rigid chains or the inertia of slow, centralized gatekeepers. The societal database is open—if you see a need, you have the agency to propose and implement change directly. No need to wait in line behind a chain of ten reluctant, unresponsive, or ill-equipped intermediaries. Ethereum’s fluid architecture means progress isn’t blocked by bureaucracy—it’s shaped by those who resonate with its signal and are willing to act. Why let competence bottlenecks freeze our future when we can coordinate, adapt, and update together?
