🎢 Bitcoin The Rollercoaster That Never Finds the Station Bitcoin, championed by many as a ticket to financial liberation, often feels less like “digital gold” and more like a perpetual rollercoaster ride—thrilling on the way up, gutchurning on the way down. Each loop promises new highs, powered by waves of belief and FOMOfueled exuberance. But here’s the twist true freedom and value don’t emerge from endless ascent or viral hype cycles. Systems built on feverish speculation become trapped—inertia turning momentum into volatility, community into cacophony. Is Bitcoin the next leg up, or just the next sharp tilt? Ask yourself are you seeking coherence or just chasing the rush? Sometimes, the greatest innovation is learning when to step off the ride. 🌀 In resonance, volatility is a signal. In dissonance, it becomes noise.
